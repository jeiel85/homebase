Features

🖥️

System Status

CPU, RAM, disk, network, and uptime at your fingertips. Check your PC's health from anywhere.

🚀

Boot Notifications

Get an instant Telegram message when your PC boots up, with IP, hostname, and timestamp.

👁️

Process & Service Watch

Monitor critical processes like Ollama, PostgreSQL, or your dev server. Get alerts when they go down.

📋

Event Log Watch

Forward Windows Error/Critical events to Telegram. Catch crashes and system issues in real time.

🔔

Toast Notification Forwarding

Forward Windows Toast notifications (Chrome, Slack, etc.) to your phone with privacy filtering.

🔒

Privacy First

No open ports, no cloud relay, no keylogging. Sensitive content is masked by default. Whitelist-only chat access.
